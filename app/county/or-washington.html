Key takeaways

  • Washington County has 600,266 residents and a median household income of $104,434, the highest income of any AI county nationwide. 46.7% of residents over 25 hold a bachelor's degree.Source: Census ACS 2023 B01003, B19013, B15003 (Washington County, OR)
  • Portland General Electric residential rates rose 29.7%, from 14.0 to 18.2 cents per kWh between 2021 and 2023, adding about $450 per year to a typical household bill. That delta lands on Hillsboro CoreWeave at the same time Intel is contracting its 16,000-employee Silicon Forest footprint.Source: data/cache/utility_rate_yoy.csv (Portland General Electric Co, OR) + sources[0] (Wikipedia: Hillsboro, OR)
  • Hillsboro is the fifth-most populous city in Oregon at 106,447 residents and the only AI county where data centers compete with Intel for fiber, power, and tax abatement. Three trans-Pacific fiber optic cable systems land in Hillsboro.Source: sources[0] (Wikipedia: Hillsboro, OR)
  • Home prices fell 1.1% over three years, the only negative 3-year home-price reading in any AI county nationwide. The CoreWeave/Flexential build is therefore the rare AI campus arriving as residential demand cools rather than accelerates.Source: data/cache/realestate_yoy.csv (FIPS 41067)
